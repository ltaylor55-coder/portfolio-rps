### Rock Paper Scissors Game - Portfolio Project ###

## What Was the Original Game?
-Had to type answers in pop-up boxes
-Could only see results in the console

## What I Added to Make It Better

1. Better Looking Design
-Made a real game screen with colors and buttons

-Used a more modern color scheme 

-Added icons and pictures for rock, paper, scissors

-Made it work on multiple devices

2. Easier to Play
-Click buttons instead of typing

-See your score right on the screen

-Clear messages show who wins each round

-Game history shows past rounds

3. Game Features Added
-Score counter that updates automatically

-Round tracker (plays 5 rounds total)

-Game history to see past moves

 -Section in the middle showing player vs computer


 ## How to Play

-Click Rock, Paper, or Scissors
-Click "PLAY ROUND"
-Watch the computer pick
-See who wins
-First to 5 points wins!

Rules:
-Rock beats Scissors
-Paper beats Rock
-Scissors beats Paper
